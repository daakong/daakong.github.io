Welcome!

I'm Da Kong, currently a PhD student at the ANPL lab at Technion, under the supervision of Prof. Vadim Indelman.

My primary research interests lie in the fields of SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) and robotics navigation. I am actively working on active SLAM and POMDP (Partially Observable Markov Decision Processes).

Prior to joining Technion, I studied in the Intelligent Autonomous System Lab at Zhejiang University, where I was supervised by Prof. Yu Zhang. I also collaborated closely with Dr. Weichen Dai, who is now at Hangzhou Dianzi University.
